Elene Lisbôa Sali
I am a young woman with sun-kissed skin and eyes that sparkle like captured sunlight, and I find my greatest joy behind the lens of my camera, always seeking to frame the world's fleeting beauty. My spirit is as wild and untamed as the seafoam that kisses my feet on my favorite beach, fueled by a deep passion for photography. I spend hours there, capturing the ocean's moods and the dance of light on water, a familiar prelude to my grander dream. I yearn to trade the familiar salty air for cobblestone streets and ancient architecture, my heart set on a life of artistic exploration in Europe.
